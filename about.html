About Stackly

India's trusted local job portal — connecting talent with top employers across Coimbatore, Tirupur, Erode and beyond.

Our Mission

Stackly was founded with a simple belief — finding a job should not be a struggle. We bridge the gap between talented professionals and great employers by making job hunting local, transparent, and efficient.

We focus on Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities where traditional job platforms fall short. Our hyper-local approach ensures that job seekers find opportunities in their own backyard and employers connect with the best local talent.

From IT parks in Peelamedu to manufacturing hubs in SIPCOT, from textile units in Tirupur to hospitals in Avinashi Road — we cover every corner of the local job market.

Our Journey

Stackly Timeline

How we grew from an idea to India's trusted local job portal

2024

Stackly is Born

Founded in Coimbatore with a vision to transform local hiring. Launched with 200+ job listings from 50 partner companies.

2025

Expansion Across Tamil Nadu

Grew to 800+ companies and 1,000+ active listings. Expanded to Tirupur, Erode, Salem, and Pollachi markets.
