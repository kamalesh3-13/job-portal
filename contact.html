FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Stackly

How do I create an account on Stackly?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the top right or visit the Register page. Fill in your details, choose whether you're a job seeker or employer, and create your free account in under 2 minutes.

Is Stackly really free for job seekers?

Absolutely! Creating a profile, browsing jobs, and applying are completely free for job seekers. You only pay if you choose to post a job as an employer.

How do I apply for a job?

Once you find a job you're interested in, click the "Apply Now" button. You can apply using your saved profile and resume with just one click, or fill out a detailed application form.

How can employers post jobs on Stackly?

Visit the "Post a Job" page, fill in your job details, select a plan, and publish. Your job will go live after a quick verification — usually within a few hours.

What areas does Stackly cover?

Stackly currently focuses on Coimbatore and nearby cities including Tirupur, Erode, Salem, and Pollachi. We're expanding to more cities across Tamil Nadu soon.
